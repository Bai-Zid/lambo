01 ALA ACTIVE AERODYNAMICS
Aerodinamica Lamborghini Attiva uses two motorized flap doors in the front splitter and a continuously adjustable rear wing. The system reacts in 200 milliseconds, adjusting aerodynamic balance based on steering angle, throttle, speed, and lateral G-force. At maximum velocity the SVJ generates 756 kg of downforce — exceeding the car's own weight. This system is the primary reason the SVJ holds the Nürburgring production car lap record.
02 NATURALLY ASPIRATED V12
Lamborghini deliberately chose not to turbocharge this engine. The 6.5L V12 revs to 8,700 RPM with zero lag between throttle input and response. Producing 770 horsepower and 720 Nm of torque, it generates more power per litre than most turbocharged engines. The combustion sound — a shrieking, rising wail from idle to redline — is considered one of the defining acoustic experiences in automotive history.
03 ISR GEARBOX 50ms SHIFTS
The 7-speed Independent Shifting Rods transmission uses dedicated actuation rods per gear instead of a shared synchromesh system. Each gear engages independently in 50 milliseconds — twice as fast as a conventional dual-clutch. In Corsa mode, upshifts feel instantaneous. There is no torque interruption. The power delivery is continuous, violent, and relentless.
04 NÜRBURGRING RECORD 6:44
On October 6, 2018, professional racing driver Marco Mapelli circled the 20.8 km Nürburgring Nordschleife in exactly 6 minutes 44.97 seconds, setting the fastest production car lap record in the circuit's history. The track features 154 corners, 300m of elevation change, and concrete barriers centimetres from the racing line. The SVJ averaged over 185 km/h for the entire 20.8 kilometres.
05 FORGED CARBON CHASSIS
The SVJ's monocoque tub is constructed from Lamborghini's proprietary forged composite — a process that combines chopped carbon fibre with resin under extreme pressure and heat. Unlike traditional woven carbon, forged composite allows complex organic shapes with equal or greater strength. Combined with an aluminium front frame, the complete car weighs just 1,525 kg despite containing a massive 6.5L V12.
